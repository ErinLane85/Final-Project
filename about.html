Erin & Penny

Erin and Penny are road dogs and love to adventure. They were both feeling restless and excited to try out the new adventure vehicle, the Sprinter Van named Vanna White.

They decided to hit the road and head North for their first adventure. Erin knew it would a challenge doing class on the road and that they were leaving a little late in the year so they couldn't go as far as they wanted, but it was still time to go on the first adventure.

Like all adventures, this one did not come without some small hiccups. The trip started with keys getting locked in a running van with Penny inside and a brake light coming on shortly into the trip. Luckily, both issues were resolved quickly and the adventure continued.

Erin and Penny traveled through numerous towns exploring, hiking, eating and enjoying the views. They met some very nice people along they way and stayed at some beautiful campsites. After a week and a half, they decided it was time to venture home to plan the next adventure. Where will they go next?
